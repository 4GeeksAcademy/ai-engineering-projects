The Artist

Based in the vibrant heart of the Pacific Northwest, Alex Rivers creates large-scale works that bridge the gap between architectural structure and organic chaos.

With over a decade of exploration in mixed media, Alex's process involves a rhythmic cycle of building and stripping away layers, mirroring the natural processes of erosion and rebirth. Each piece is a meditation on the passage of time.
